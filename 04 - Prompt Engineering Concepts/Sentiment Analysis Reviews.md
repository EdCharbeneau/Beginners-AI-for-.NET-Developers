## Review 1:

By and large, one of my favorite beans! I do a little happy dance when I receive them in the mail. I like to roast right at second crack. It makes for a delightfully rich cup with some subtle spicy undertones.

## Review 2:

I first ordered this for a friend who likes his coffee extra dark. Isn’t the most attractive but it’s superb flavor profile will more than make up for it.

## Review 3

This is a very strong coffee and lingering unpleasant  aftertaste. It's unusual for me, because I tend to like lighter roasts.
